Welcome to SANIPIPE ENGINEERING SERVICES

Sanipipe Engineering Services is a leader in providing comprehensive engineering solutions, leveraging extensive experience and expertise to deliver a 'Total Engineering Service'. With a steadfast commitment to quality, cost-competitiveness, and punctual project delivery, we have earned the trust of clients across local and international markets, particularly in South Africa. As a Level 1 BBBEE-certified company, Sanipipe is proud to be a mature, privately owned organization with a stable and highly skilled workforce. We specialize in Mechanical Fabrication and Installations, catering to diverse industries including Food & Beverage, Pharmaceutical, Oil and Gas, and Water Treatment. We are accredited with ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 3834 certifications, demonstrating our dedication to adhering to the highest industry standards. Our expertise spans both local regulations and international benchmarks, including SANS 347, ASME BPE, AWS D18.1, AWS D1.1, ASME B31.3, B31.5, B31.8, ASME VIII & PD5500, and ASME IX. Our specialized welding procedures are qualified to ASME and EN codes, and we possess extensive capabilities in welding both carbon steel and stainless steel. Each project benefits from our tailored approach, ensuring all welding procedures meet specific requirements and industry standards. At Sanipipe Engineering Services, we are committed to exceeding expectations, providing innovative engineering solutions, and supporting sustainable growth through technical excellence and a customer-first approach.
